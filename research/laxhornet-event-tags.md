# LaxHornet Event and Tag Recommendations

These recommendations are intentionally tag-first. The research supports tracking observable behaviors and decisions, but many are too subjective for one-handed live tracking.

| Name | Definition | Parent-friendly description | When recorded | Objective/subjective | Positive/negative indicators | Examples | Related events |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Created Advantage | Player action forced the defense to react or gave the team a better option. | Player action forced the defense to react or gave the team a better option. | Record after a dodge, feed, clear, or off-ball action creates space, slide, or numbers. | Subjective | Positive when it reflects repeatable process; negative only when paired with poor-decision or immediate-loss tags. | Use as a postgame event tag, not a required live stat. | assist, shotOnGoal, smartPlay |
| Extended Possession | Player kept the possession alive after pressure, a loose ball, or a contested situation. | Player kept the possession alive after pressure, a loose ball, or a contested situation. | Record after backup, ground ball, ride, clear support, or safe reset. | Mostly objective with judgment | Positive when it reflects repeatable process; negative only when paired with poor-decision or immediate-loss tags. | Use as a postgame event tag, not a required live stat. | backedUpShot, groundBall, successfulClear, smartPlay |
| Supported Ball Carrier | Player provided a passing outlet, screen, verbal help, or spacing that helped a teammate. | Player provided a passing outlet, screen, verbal help, or spacing that helped a teammate. | Record when an off-ball player makes the ball carrier's next decision easier. | Subjective | Positive when it reflects repeatable process; negative only when paired with poor-decision or immediate-loss tags. | Use as a postgame event tag, not a required live stat. | smartPlay, assist, successfulClear |
| Forced Defensive Rotation | Player action made the opponent slide, rotate, or lose shape. | Player action made the opponent slide, rotate, or lose shape. | Record on dodges, skip passes, hard cuts, or off-ball movement that changes the defense. | Subjective | Positive when it reflects repeatable process; negative only when paired with poor-decision or immediate-loss tags. | Use as a postgame event tag, not a required live stat. | assist, shotOnGoal, smartPlay |
| Recognized Transition | Player saw numbers, danger, or unsettled opportunity early. | Player saw numbers, danger, or unsettled opportunity early. | Record when player quickly pushes, supports, or slows transition appropriately. | Subjective | Positive when it reflects repeatable process; negative only when paired with poor-decision or immediate-loss tags. | Use as a postgame event tag, not a required live stat. | successfulClear, smartPlay, hustlePlay |
| Protected the Middle | Defensive player kept attack away from dangerous central space. | Defensive player kept attack away from dangerous central space. | Record on defensive stops, forced weak-hand dodges, or smart positioning. | Subjective | Positive when it reflects repeatable process; negative only when paired with poor-decision or immediate-loss tags. | Use as a postgame event tag, not a required live stat. | defensiveStop, causedTurnover |
| Recovered After Mistake | Player responded constructively after an error. | Player responded constructively after an error. | Record after turnover, missed shot, or failed clear followed by hustle, ride, GB, or smart reset. | Subjective | Positive when it reflects repeatable process; negative only when paired with poor-decision or immediate-loss tags. | Use as a postgame event tag, not a required live stat. | turnover, hustlePlay, causedTurnover |
| Good Decision, Unsuccessful Execution | The idea/read was sound but the execution failed. | The idea/read was sound but the execution failed. | Record when a pass, shot, clear, or defensive play is appropriate but incomplete. | Subjective | Positive when it reflects repeatable process; negative only when paired with poor-decision or immediate-loss tags. | Use as a postgame event tag, not a required live stat. | turnover, failedClear, shot |
| Poor Decision, Successful Outcome | The outcome was positive, but the decision was risky or low percentage. | The outcome was positive, but the decision was risky or low percentage. | Record sparingly when a goal/pass/clear works despite poor process. | Subjective | Positive when it reflects repeatable process; negative only when paired with poor-decision or immediate-loss tags. | Use as a postgame event tag, not a required live stat. | goal, assist, successfulClear |
| Won the Second Effort | Player stayed in the play after the first attempt and helped regain/keep advantage. | Player stayed in the play after the first attempt and helped regain/keep advantage. | Record after rebounds, loose balls, backups, rides, or recovery plays. | Mostly objective | Positive when it reflects repeatable process; negative only when paired with poor-decision or immediate-loss tags. | Use as a postgame event tag, not a required live stat. | groundBall, hustlePlay, backedUpShot |
| Communicated Early | Player gave useful verbal/visual information before pressure arrived. | Player gave useful verbal/visual information before pressure arrived. | Record on clears, defensive rotations, goalie outlets, and unsettled play. | Subjective | Positive when it reflects repeatable process; negative only when paired with poor-decision or immediate-loss tags. | Use as a postgame event tag, not a required live stat. | smartPlay, successfulClear, defensiveStop |
| Stayed Composed Under Pressure | Player made a simple next play while pressured or after a high-emotion moment. | Player made a simple next play while pressured or after a high-emotion moment. | Record during close/late game, after turnover, or on clears. | Subjective | Positive when it reflects repeatable process; negative only when paired with poor-decision or immediate-loss tags. | Use as a postgame event tag, not a required live stat. | smartPlay, successfulClear |
