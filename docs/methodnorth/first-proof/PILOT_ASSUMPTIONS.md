## Implications For LaxHornet

- The feature must remain behind a default-off flag.
- The user experience must preserve uncertainty.
- The parent experience must not imply evaluation authority.
- The coach experience must not imply that the system can replace coach judgment.
- Any evidence-correction feature must be honest about what is recorded, what was corrected, and what remains unsupported by the current data model.
