For Presidents, Directors, and Officers

Make player development a visible program benefit.

LaxHornet gives a lacrosse organization something families can use every week: a practical way to understand effort, possession, decisions, and progress after games.

Why It Matters

Give families a clearer record of what was tracked.

Club and youth programs compete on coaching, culture, communication, and trust. LaxHornet supports that workflow by organizing recorded contributions and optional review prompts separately from rankings, highlight reels, and final scores.

Review

Families can revisit recorded moments

Each game keeps the event timeline, selected contribution categories, and one optional next focus together.

Differentiation

A clear program add-on

Offer a modern development tool without forcing coaches to manage another platform during games.

Communication

Better post-game language

Optional recaps separate recorded contributions, cautious interpretation, a conversation prompt, and an optional focus.

Program Outcomes

Useful for a pilot team, a club-wide value add, or a seasonal development initiative.

LaxHornet can start small and grow with the organization. A single team can test the workflow, then directors can decide whether to make it part of parent education, player development, or team communication across age groups.
